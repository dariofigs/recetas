• 2 servings
  • 5 minutes

basil spinach pesto

ingredients

  • 4 cups basil 2sih packets from the HEB
  • 1⁄2 cup spinach
  • 1⁄4 cup parsley
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1⁄3 cup pine nuts
  • 2⁄3 cup grated parmesean
  • 1⁄2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp black pepper
  • 3⁄4 cup olive oil
  • zest of one lemon
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

instructions

Add all ingredients to a food processor/blender and process until completely smooth.
